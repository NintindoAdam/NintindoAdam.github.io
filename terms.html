2. Service Description

TimeBox is a minimalist iOS time management app that helps users organize daily schedules using time-blocking. Features include:

  • Daily planning with up to three top priority tasks and unlimited secondary tasks
  • Time block scheduling with customizable start times and durations
  • Task management to track completion and progress
  • Brain Dump space for capturing thoughts and ideas
  • Archive to review historical daily plans
  • Local notifications for scheduled time blocks
  • Calendar view of daily schedules
  • Local data storage on your device
