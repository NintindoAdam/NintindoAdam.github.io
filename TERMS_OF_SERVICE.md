## 2. Service Description

TimeBox is a minimalist iOS time management application designed to help users organize their daily schedules using time-blocking techniques. The App provides the following features:

- **Daily Planning**: Create and manage daily plans with up to 3 top priority tasks and unlimited secondary tasks
- **Time Block Scheduling**: Schedule time blocks with customizable start times and durations
- **Task Management**: Track task completion and progress throughout your day
- **Brain Dump**: Capture and organize thoughts and ideas in a dedicated space
- **Archive**: Review and access historical daily plans
- **Local Notifications**: Receive reminders for scheduled time blocks
- **Calendar View**: Visual representation of your daily schedule
- **Local Data Storage**: All user data is stored locally on your device

The App is designed following Apple's Human Interface Guidelines with a focus on minimalism, privacy, and ease of use.
