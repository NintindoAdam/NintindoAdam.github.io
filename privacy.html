Privacy Policy
Version: 1.0 · Effective Date: January 3, 2026 · Developer: Adam Markocki (Krakow, Poland) · Contact: markockidev@icloud.com

What Data We Collect

Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Username (optional)
  • Tasks, time blocks, daily plans
  • Brain dump notes
  • Preferences (theme, language), schedule settings, notification preferences
  • Onboarding completion status

Storage: UserDefaults and JSON files on your device only.

Not Collected

  • Email or personal identifiers
  • Location data, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, health data
  • Analytics, tracking data, device identifiers, crash reports

How We Use Your Data

  • App functionality: manage and display tasks
  • Personalization: remember preferences
  • Notifications: local reminders (with permission)
  • Purchases: process voluntary tip jar donations via Apple

All processing happens locally on your device.

Data Sharing

We share zero data. Your data never leaves your device. No servers, cloud, or third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • StoreKit for tip jar purchases (Apple handles payment)
  • UserNotifications for local reminders (on-device)

No third-party SDKs, analytics, or tracking.

Your Rights

  • Access and edit data within the app
  • Delete data in-app or by uninstalling
  • Control notifications
  • Export via iCloud/iTunes backups (JSON format)

Data Security

  • Data stored in iOS sandbox, protected by device encryption
  • No network transmission; no external servers

Data Deletion

  1. Delete individual items in the app
  2. Clear plans via DataManager
  3. Uninstall the app for permanent deletion

Privacy Compliance

  • GDPR: rights to access, delete, portability
  • CCPA/CPRA: right to know and delete
  • COPPA: safe for all ages; no data collection
  • Accurate App Store privacy labels

Children's Privacy

Safe for children under 13: no personal information collected; no tracking or advertising; data stays on device; parents can delete all data.

In-App Purchases

Tip Jar (voluntary donations) processed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not see or store payment info; no features are unlocked by purchases.

Notifications

Task reminders and weekly support reminders are generated locally. All notifications can be disabled in settings; no data leaves the device.

International Users

No data transfers occur; data never leaves your device. Compliant with GDPR (EU), CCPA/CPRA (California), and privacy-by-design globally.

App Store Privacy Labels

Data Not Collected; Data Not Tracked; No Data Linked to You.

Updates to This Policy

We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notice and by updating the Last Updated date.
